Installation:
Extract with paths into your Half-life folder.

If that fails here's where all the files should go:
(If a folder don't exist create it.)
cstrike\gfx\env\ .tga (six files.)
cstrike\maps\ .bsp, .res .txt (four files)
cstrike\models\mountainhamlet\ .mdl (ten files)
cstrike\sound\ambience\mountainhamlet\ .wav (eighteen files)
cstrike\PODBot\WPTDefault\ .pwf (one file)

Known Issues:

Very tight at some places.
Uses 4mb texture memmory. (not the 2mb as is HL standard)
Takes long time to load. (due to lots of textures and enteties)
Annoying edges you constantly fall to your death from ;-)
